# About me

I am a Senior Software Engineer at Google, where I work on Factuality for Gemini through post-training interventions.
My work sits at the intersection of research and engineering — I enjoy turning ideas from research into products that work at scale.

Before Google, I spent several years at IBM Research and BenevolentAI applying machine learning to problems in chemistry and drug discovery.
I hold a Ph.D. from ETH Zurich, where I worked on interactive approaches for quantum chemistry.
